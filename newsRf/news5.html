How to save money for bachelor's and master's degrees?
20, december 2021, 17:10
836

Aqyl deposit is designed to save money for the child's future higher education.

Educational contribution

The state education savings system will allow citizens to open Aqyl educational deposits in the future to pay for tuition in technical and vocational, post-secondary, higher and postgraduate (master's) educational programs in Kazakhstan.

The accumulation system is the most interesting component. Much depends on the timing of savings, government assistance, and the person's willingness to increase their savings. The advantage of an educational deposit is that you can make voluntary contributions at any time and in any amount.

The educational deposit is unique in that it includes a premium of 5% from the state for the entire amount of the deposit, taking into account not only bank interest, but also the bank's interest. The state premium for children with disabilities from large and low-income families, orphans, children left without parental care, is 7%.

What are the advantages?

Educational deposit is subject to mandatory guarantee of deposits of individuals. This means that if you lose the license of the bank where you opened such a deposit, the money will be reimbursed to you, as they are guaranteed by the Deposit Insurance Fund of Kazakhstan. The maximum amount of payment is 10 million tenge.

The educational deposit is a special account and is protected from any withdrawals by third parties. It is not banned or blocked.

You can open a Aqyl deposit in your own name or in the name of the child. Conveniently, the depositor can only be a person who wants to study and get an education. The main thing is to use the funds only for bachelor's and master's degrees. And if you or your child enrolls in a grant, you can receive all the savings, including bank fees and state bonuses, or spend the money to pay for a master's degree or transfer it to a third party.

Is it possible to withdraw money ahead of time?

Yes, it can be done while keeping the bank's interest, but at the same time the state premium is lost, which is returned to the budget.

Requirements for educational deposits in the participating banks of the SSS:
  • annual effective interest rate (ANI) - up to 10%;
  • state premium - 5 or 7% (7% - for orphans, children left without parental care, the disabled, children from large families and low-income families; 5% - for other categories of the population of the Republic of Kazakhstan).
  • The state premium is calculated annually for the entire amount of the deposit, taking into account the bank's interest.
