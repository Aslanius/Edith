3,500 applicants received vacant grants to study in Kazakhstani universities
31, december 2021, 17:06
3678

From the second semester, grant holders can study at universities for free.

About 3,500 applicants became holders of vacant grants to study in Kazakhstani universities, the press service of the Ministry of Education and Science said.

State educational grants were distributed among applicants who took part in the competition, which took place this summer.

Applicants will be trained in such areas as "Pedagogical Sciences", "Information and Communication Technologies", "Engineering, Manufacturing and Construction Industries", "Agriculture and Bioresources".

"The ranking was carried out in accordance with the points scored at the summer UNT, that is, grants were awarded on a competitive basis based on the results of the available UNT certificates. I note that the procedure is fully automated. And now, from the second semester, grant holders can study at universities for free. they do not need to submit any documents, since they submitted all the necessary documents in the summer, "said Adlet Toybaev, director of the department of higher and postgraduate education of the Ministry of Education and Science of the Republic of Kazakhstan.

Grant holders can download grant certificates from the National Testing Center website. It is necessary to enter IIN and ICT (individual code of the tested person).
