Persons who have graduation documents from a general secondary institution, vocational or technical institution, or a higher institution are admitted to universities in Kazakhstan. Applicants have the opportunity to study both at their own expense (paid education) and at the expense of public funds (receiving all kinds of grants). In addition to residents of the Republic of Kazakhstan, persons of Kazakh nationality who do not live in Kazakhstan, as well as persons without specific citizenship who live in the country, have the right to receive grants.

To enter educational institutions, in the Republic of Kazakhstan, you must pass the UNT (Unified National Testing) or CTA (Comprehensive Testing of the Applicant). CTA passes:

  1. graduates who did not pass the UNT due to the fact that they graduated from school before its introduction;
  2. those who graduated from vocational or technical schools;
  3. those who graduated from school abroad;
  4. those who graduated from school, where training was conducted in any language except Russian or Kazakh.

In order for a foreigner to enter the KZ-university, he needs to go through an interview. Some segments of the population receive quotas for higher education. Enrollment is carried out by the selection committee. For admission, you must collect a certain number of points.

Places of Kazakhstani universities in the QS EECA-2022 ranking

30, december 2021, 13:35
673

The ranking includes universities in Europe and Central Asia, classified as "developing".

British agency Quacquarelli Symonds has updated the ranking of universities in developing countries in Europe and Central Asia. The QS EECA-2022 ranking includes 34 Kazakhstani universities - seven more than a year earlier.

The first place was taken by the Lomonosov Moscow State University, the second and third - by the Prague Charles University and the St. Petersburg State University. Of the Kazakh universities, the highest, 16th place was taken by the Kazakh National University named after Al-Farabi.

Among other domestic universities in the top 100 ranking were:

  • Eurasian National University. L. Gumilyov (42nd place);
  • Satpayev University (64th place);
  • Kazakh National Pedagogical University named after Abay (70th place);
  • Kazakh National Agrarian University (79th place);
  • South Kazakhstan University named after M. Auezova (96th place);
  • Kazakh-British Technical University (100th place).

Universities in the ranking are assessed by 10 factors, including academic reputation, reputation as an employer, the ratio of teachers and students, research activity of teachers, international research cooperation, activity on the Internet, the share of doctors of science among employees, the level of citation of works published by employees, the number of foreign teachers and students.
